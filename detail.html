General description

At the end of a cypress avenue protected by the woods, the ‘curtain’ of the Umbrian hills opens, a real postcard image that surrounds Alla Fattoria, including its guest houses. This very large family villa is near Todi, at walking distance to Doglio (two kilometres distance), an ancient hamlet where you can find a grocery store and a coffee shop. This exclusive property with a private spa and swimming pool is the ideal venue for weddings, by renting Alla Fattoria, you will be able to make your dream come true by experiencing a fabulous wedding, whilst enjoying a romantic, relaxing and authentic Italian holiday. Discover Alla Fattoria wedding packages and choose the best combination suiting your needs and dreams! The whole property, with its two acres of land, partly garden and partly olive groves looks out over the Natural Park of Monte Peglia which can be admired from almost every room. To forget about the smog, the traffic noise, the cases of modern civilization is easy in this place, where nature infuses a sense of peace, involving the five senses with its colours, scents and sounds.
